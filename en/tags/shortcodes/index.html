Violin and Piano Duets

I love to play duets with myself, as I can play both violin and piano. I’ve been doing this for 3 years, and love the process every time. I find a song that is meaningful to me, and then I find sheet music for both violin and piano. Sometimes if there isn’t an existing part for violin, I have to compose my own. I did this for my “The Second Waltz” duet.
